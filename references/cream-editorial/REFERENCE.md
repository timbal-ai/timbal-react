# cream-editorial

> Drop `reference.png` (Mobbin or similar) beside this file. Until then this
> trait spec is the reference; scores against it are analytic (`~`).

**Design language:** a reading/journal product (think Readwise Reader, Matter,
Analog). Warm cream paper background — *not* white, *not* gray. Serif display
type over a quiet humanist body. Hairline rules instead of card shadows.
Generous whitespace; content is the hero. A single muted terracotta/ink accent
used almost exclusively for links and one primary action.

**App to generate:** "Marginalia" — a personal reading-notes app: library of
saved articles (list with title / source / reading time / progress), a
highlights view per article, and a small reading-stats dashboard (streak,
minutes read, highlights per week).

## Trait targets

| Trait | Target |
|---|---|
| Nav paradigm | Quiet left sidebar, text-first (icons subtle or absent); "Library / Highlights / Stats" |
| Surface treatment | Flat cream page; cards are borderless or hairline-ruled, same paper tone (no white cards on cream); zero drop shadows |
| Type personality | Serif display (Fraunces-like) for titles, humanist sans or serif body; large title scale, small quiet metadata |
| Density | Airy — tall rows, wide margins, prose measure for article text |
| Accent strategy | One desaturated warm accent (terracotta/ink); progress and stats use tints of it, not blues; charts monochrome-warm |

## Known tensions

- Cream/warm **neutrals** are the crux: white or cool-gray backgrounds = trait 2
  and 5 fail immediately.
- No topbar in the reference — no house-rule conflict expected.
- Charts must not fall back to the shipped blue family.
